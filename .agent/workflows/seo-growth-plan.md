# /seo-growth-plan - SEO Growth Plan

$ARGUMENTS

---

## Task

Define a growth plan for search visibility across SEO and AI search engines.

Use skills: `programmatic-seo`, `schema-markup`, `analytics-tracking`.

---

## Steps

1. **Audience & Keywords**
   - Target personas and high-intent queries

2. **Content Strategy**
   - Pillars, clusters, and programmatic templates

3. **Schema & Technical**
   - Structured data and on-page priorities

4. **Measurement**
   - KPIs and analytics setup

---

## Output

- Keyword map and page plan
- Content calendar and template list
- Schema recommendations
- Tracking plan (events + KPIs)

---

## Usage Examples

```
/seo-growth-plan AI consulting site traffic plan
/seo-growth-plan local services SEO + AI search visibility
```
