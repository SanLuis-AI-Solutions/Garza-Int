# /conversion-optimization - Conversion Optimization

$ARGUMENTS

---

## Task

Optimize lead capture and sales conversion through form and funnel improvements.

Use skills: `form-cro`, `analytics-tracking`.

---

## Steps

1. **Funnel Review**
   - Current pages, drop-off points, and bottlenecks

2. **Form Improvements**
   - Field minimization, copy, trust signals, UX

3. **Experiment Ideas**
   - Prioritized tests and hypotheses

4. **Measurement**
   - Event tracking and success criteria

---

## Output

- Funnel audit summary
- CRO recommendations (ranked)
- Test plan and tracking checklist

---

## Usage Examples

```
/conversion-optimization improve lead form on homepage
/conversion-optimization optimize booking flow
```
