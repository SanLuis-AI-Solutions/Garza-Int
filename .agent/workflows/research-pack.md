# /research-pack
# Audience + keyword + competitor + brand identity bundle

$ARGUMENTS

---

## Task

Run an end‑to‑end research bundle for fast strategy alignment.

---

## Steps

0. **Load Project Brief (if present)**
   - Read `PROJECT_BRIEF.md` and use it to prefill context
   - Only ask for missing or unclear items

1. **Audience Research**
   - Personas, pains, jobs‑to‑be‑done

2. **Keyword & Competitor Research**
   - Primary keywords, intent clusters
   - Competitor gaps and content opportunities

3. **Brand Identity Snapshot**
   - Positioning + voice + messaging pillars

4. **Actionable Outputs**
   - Prioritized recommendations and next steps

---

## Output

- Audience summary
- Keyword map + intent clusters
- Competitor gap summary
- Brand identity snapshot
- Next‑step plan

---

## Usage

```
/research-pack new service business
```
