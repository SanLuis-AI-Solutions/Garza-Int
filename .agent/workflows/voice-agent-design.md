# /voice-agent-design - Voice Agent Design

$ARGUMENTS

---

## Task

Design a voice agent experience aligned to business goals and constraints.

Use skills: `voice-agents`.

---

## Steps

1. **Use Case & Scope**
   - Target calls, outcomes, escalation rules

2. **Conversation Flow**
   - Intents, prompts, and fallback paths

3. **Integrations**
   - CRM, scheduling, ticketing, or internal tools

4. **Compliance**
   - Recording, consent, and privacy

---

## Output

- Voice agent scope and KPIs
- Conversation flow outline
- Integration requirements
- Risk and compliance notes

---

## Usage Examples

```
/voice-agent-design inbound lead qualification
/voice-agent-design appointment scheduling assistant
```
