# /discovery - Client Discovery

$ARGUMENTS

---

## Task

Run a structured discovery to clarify the business problem and define measurable outcomes.

Use skills: `ai-product`, `prompt-engineer`, `analytics-tracking`.

---

## Steps

1. **Business Goals**
   - What outcome is most valuable? (leads, sales, retention, ops efficiency)
   - Timeframe and budget range

2. **Audience & Context**
   - Who is the user and what is their job-to-be-done?
   - Current process, pain points, and existing tools

3. **Constraints & Risks**
   - Data access, compliance, privacy, integrations, timeline risks

4. **Success Metrics**
   - Define KPIs and how they will be measured

---

## Output

- Problem statement
- Success metrics (KPIs)
- Constraints and assumptions
- Solution hypotheses (1-3)
- Next-step recommendations

---

## Usage Examples

```
/discovery new AI lead-gen system for local services
/discovery automate onboarding and internal ops
```
